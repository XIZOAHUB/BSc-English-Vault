## 📂 Repository Structure

Here is a quick breakdown of the modules covered in this vault. Click on any section to dive into the detailed notes:

### 🗣️ [01. Phonetics](./01-Phonetics)
Mastering the mechanics of spoken English and pronunciation.
*   **Word Accent:** Syllable stress and Noun/Verb shifts.
*   **Connected Speech:** Rhythm, weak/strong forms, and the schwa sound.
*   **Intonation:** Rising (Tune 2) and Falling (Tune 1) pitches.
*   **IPA:** Phonemic transcriptions for common words.

### 🧠 [02. Grammar Core Concepts](./02-Grammar-Core-Concepts)
Understanding structural rules and fixing foundational errors.
*   Comparatives and Superlatives (Fixing double marking).
*   Active and Passive Voice transformations.
*   Fixing Dangling Participles.
*   Using "One" as a prop-word.
*   Correlatives and Parallelism.

### ✍️ [03. Grammar Usage & Errors](./03-Grammar-Usage-and-Errors)
Context-based usage and avoiding common conversational mistakes.
*   Removing Redundancies and fixing Prepositions.
*   **Confusing Pairs:** Who vs. Whom, Many vs. Much, Still vs. Yet, So that vs. So as, Make vs. Do.

### 🤝 [04. Communication Etiquette](./04-Communication-Etiquette)
*   **Courtesy Words:** Proper placement of *please*, *thank you*, and other professional pleasantries.

### 📝 [05. Practice & Quizzes](./05-Practice-and-Quizzes)
*   Phonetics Practice Sets (Transcription and Stress marking).
*   Grammar Correction Tests (Fill-in-the-blanks and error spotting).
